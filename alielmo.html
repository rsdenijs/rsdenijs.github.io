Story Book

Ali is a girl who likes to have fun.

One day, Ali went for a walk in the park.

She saw a funny looking red creature playing with a ball.

Ali went up to Elmo and asked if she could play too.

Elmo said yes and they had a great time playing together.

After they were tired of playing, Ali and Elmo sat down on a bench.

Ali pulled out some crayons and a coloring book from her backpack.

She asked Elmo if he wanted to color with her.

Elmo happily agreed and they spent the rest of the afternoon coloring and laughing.

Ali and Elmo had a lot of fun together and couldn't wait to hang out again.
