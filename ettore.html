Story Book

Ettore was a curious boy who loved trains and bikes.

He spent every day riding his bike to the train station to watch the trains go by.

One day, Ettore's bike got a flat tire.

Ettore was disappointed, but he didn't let that stop him.

He used his toolkit to fix the tire and was back on the road in no time.

Ettore arrived at the train station, just in time to see the train he had been waiting for.

He waved at the conductor as the train passed by.

Ettore was so happy to have fixed his bike and see the train, he rode home with a big smile on his face.
