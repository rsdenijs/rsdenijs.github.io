Story Book

Tim, Rod, Luk, Jenn, and Ben are computer engineers who work together at a technology company.

One day, they decide to go on an adventure to discover the true meaning of Christmas.

As they travel, they encounter all sorts of crazy things, like talking reindeer and mischievous elves.

Despite the challenges they face, the group remains determined to find the true meaning of Christmas.

After many adventures and mishaps, they finally arrive at the North Pole.

There, they meet Santa Claus and learn the true meaning of Christmas: it's about spending time with loved ones and spreading joy to others.

Feeling inspired and grateful, the group returns home and decides to spend Christmas together as a family.

And so, the computer engineers discover that Christmas is not just about the gifts, but about the love and joy that they share with one another.
