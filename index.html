My Story

Erik was a little boy with a big love for firetrucks. He loved everything about them - the bright red color, the loud sirens, and the brave firemen who rode in them.
Every day, Erik would watch the firetrucks race down the street, sirens blaring, on their way to put out a fire or save someone in need.
One day, Erik's dream came true. A fireman came to his school to talk about fire safety, and at the end of the presentation, he invited Erik and his classmates to take a ride in the firetruck.
Erik could hardly contain his excitement as he climbed into the firetruck and put on a fireman's helmet. The fireman showed him how to use the hose and taught him about all the equipment on the truck.
It was the best day of Erik's life. He made so many new friends on the firetruck, and he even got to help put out a small fire in a nearby park.
From that day on, Erik knew he wanted to be a fireman when he grew up. He spent every day learning more about fire safety and practicing with the hose, determined to be the best fireman he could be.
